My Design Process

My design process starts with understanding and empathy. It is about searching the right question and problem that needs to be solved. We as designers are designing for businesses and users, which why we have to think about both of them while focusing on the problem and generating a solution.

Empathize

Connecting with a plethora of potential users through conducting interviews, understanding the users emotions and feelings toward the product and seeking their stories and understanding whilst using the product.

Define

Understanding the issues of human centric design, identifying meaningful and exciting tensions users experience within the product, generating insights gained from the user research processes of the product analysis.

Ideate

Brainstorming ideas and several concepts for the product development process and building upon the interviewee's input and insights on the product design.

Prototype

Developing low and high fidelity iterations of the product, creating functioning UI components of the design and referring back to user's input and insights of how and where the product's design is helping the user.

Test

Assessing the product's beta through design, functionality and infrastructure with the input of the user, embracing minor to extensive changes of the design if necessary.
